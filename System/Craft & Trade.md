## Materials
Crafting equipment can be done with **materials**, which can be gained through session rewards, events, and downtime foraging. Each material come assigned with one or more **tags**, which correlate to the qualities you are able to craft into your equipment. Each tag is associated with a symbol.

Each tag is associated with either an **effect**, **element**, or **rarity**. 
* Effects are tags that are associated with mechanical changes, ex. `ğŸ”ºsharp` adding additional damage to a weapon. Materials should always have one of these tags.
* Elements are tags associated with a damage type, ex. `âš¡bolt`. These elements can also be correlated to a status effect, Dazed in the case of `âš¡bolt`. A material lacking one of these tags is considered unattuned.
* Rarity tags are associated with the [[Player Progression#Player Tiers|player tier]] they are attributed to, such as `ğŸŒ€strange` representing the second tier, levels 10-19. A material lacking one of these tags is considered **common** or otherwise basic.
